The Advertising


Big Mac - The Village People
The lyrics, "Nothing's better than a big mac," really serve to address the issue. It is truly a fast food nation. The Village People didn't sing about just anything. They sung about the Big Mac, the epitome of fast food. Created using the principles of an assembly line with the cheapest ingredients, by the cheapest labor, for as cheap as possible. Glorious.

Other Works
The documentary Super Size Me covers a month of Morgan Spurlock's life as he eats only McDonald's fast food. Both works cover the fast food industry, Fast Food Nation from the background, Super Size Me from the foreground, literally the eating of the food and what it does to a person.


The fast food industry is glorified, mostly through advertising. Though the reality is much worse.
